Mingle. Drink. Enjoy. Repeat.

The Chicago Polyglot Mingle is for software developers and designers who enjoy socializing with like-minded achievers. Come on out for free drinks and appetizers. Meet new colleagues. It's a party. Enjoy!

We'll gather at Sully's House, 1501 North Dayton, about two blocks away from the Apple Store at North & Clybourn. July is a month for nice weather, so get ready to enjoy the rooftop!
